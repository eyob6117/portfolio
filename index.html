About me

Eyob Teklu is an Integration Support Engineer at Safaricom Ethiopia, a leading telecom organization in Ethiopia. He is passionate about building websites and applications for small projects and clients. Eyob holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering with honors from Haramaya University. In addition to his professional endeavors, he is dedicated to advancing his skills in cloud computing. Eyob is proud to have achieved certification in AWS Cloud, showcasing his expertise in cloud technologies. Currently, he is actively pursuing the AWS Certified Solutions Architect certification, further solidifying his commitment to staying at the forefront of cloud architecture and design. Before joining Safaricom, Eyob contributed to the digital transformation of a local bank called Awas Bank, where he served as a Digital Channel Officer. His diverse experience in both telecommunications and banking reflects a well-rounded understanding of the intersection of technology and business. Looking ahead, Eyob aspires to establish his own tech startup within the next 5 years, leveraging his skills and passion for innovation. Beyond the tech realm, he values personal growth and practices meditation, finding inspiration and creativity through inner journeys and observations."
